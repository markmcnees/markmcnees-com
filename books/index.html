The Forever Founder

Timeless leadership principles from the founder of history's most enduring organization.

The Forever Founder studies the most enduring organization in history and the leadership principles that built it. Mark McNees has spent his career as a serial founder, a professor of entrepreneurship, and the builder of organizations that thrive beyond his tenure. The book distills 2,000 years of organizational survival into practical lessons for founders and executives. It covers how to build culture that scales, develop people who lead without you, and design an institution that endures past its founder. Written for leaders who want durable principles, not passing tactics.
